PARMIGIANO


  • Region: Italy
  • Milk: Cow
  • Texture: Hard

Parmigiano Reggiano is a hard cheese that originates from Italy. Known for its crunchy texture and rich nutty flavor, the cheese is used in a variety of dishes such as pastas, soups and salads. In order to be classified as authentic parmigiano, the production process must follow the Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) standards.
