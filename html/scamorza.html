SCAMORZA


  • Region: Italy
  • Milk: Cow
  • Texture: Semi-Soft

Scamorza cheese is a semi soft Italian cheese known for its mild and slightly sweet flavor. It follows the same production process as mozzarella, where the curd is stretched and moulded until it reaches an elastic texture. The distinct teardrop shape is formed when a string is wrapped around the cheese to dry. Scamorza comes in different varieties such as fresh, aged and smoked. Smoked scamorza comes in a brown/yellowish color while fresh scamorza is white.
