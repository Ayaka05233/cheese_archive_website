COLBY JACK


  • Region: United States
  • Milk: Cow
  • Texture: Semi-Hard

Colby Jack is a semi-hard cheese that is made from Colby and Monterey Jack. This mixture of cheese creates its signature marbling appearance. Mainly produced in Wisconsin USA, Colby Jack is known for its springy texture and mellow flavor and is used in various dishes such as soups, sandwiches and burgers.
