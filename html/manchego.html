MANCHEGO


  • Region: Spain
  • Milk: Sheep
  • Texture: Hard

Manchego is a traditional spanish cheese named after the region of La Mancha. Known for its nutty and caramel flavor, the cheese comes in different varieties which are classified by the age duration. The signature zigzag pattern on the rind comes from the grass baskets in which the cheese are placed in. Today, a similar effect is achieved through plastic molds.
