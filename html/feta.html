FETA


  • Region: Greece
  • Milk: Sheep
  • Texture: Soft

Originating from Greece, Feta is a soft tangy cheese traditionally made from pasteurized sheep’s or goat’s milk. Today, most mass produced feta contains cow’s milk. Due to its high sodium content and tangy flavor, feta is a staple ingredient in many Mediterranean dishes such as pasta, pizza and salads.
