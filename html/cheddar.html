CHEDDAR


  • Region: United Kingdom
  • Milk: Cow
  • Texture: Hard

Cheddar is a hard cheese that dates back to the 12th century and originates from the village of Cheddar, England. Due to its long shelf life and portability, it was a popular food amongst soldiers and travellers. Today, cheddar is a popular cheese that comes in various flavors, textures and colors.
