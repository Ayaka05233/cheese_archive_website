ASIAGO


  • Region: Italy
  • Milk: Cow
  • Texture: Hard

Asiago is a hard Italian cheese that dates back to more than a thousand years. The rind, or outer surface of the cheese is yellowish brown while the interior is covered with irregular holes. It is DOP certified, meaning the cheese can only be produced in the regions of Veneto and Trentino to be considered authentic. Traditionally made from sheep’s milk, the flavor and texture of Asiago varies depending on the aging period.
