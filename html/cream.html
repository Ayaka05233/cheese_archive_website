CREAM CHEESE


  • Region: United States
  • Milk: Cow
  • Texture: Soft

Originating from Philadelphia, cream cheese is a fresh soft cheese made from either cream or a mixture of cream and milk. It is high in fat content and is used for frostings, dips and pastries. Unlike aged cheese, cream cheese has a short shelf life and comes in a variety of flavors such as fruits, garlic and herbs.
